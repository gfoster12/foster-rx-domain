Methodology note

Cohort. Twenty AD/ADRD protein targets prioritized from a broader 35-target screen. Source data integrated across CanSAR network drugability, Probe Miner chemistry exposure (June 2021 dump), Agora target-nominator metadata, and a 2026 public-source FTO & competitive-landscape review.

Composite score (0–1). Four weighted dimensions: A · ADRD evidence from primary literature, AMP-AD nominations, and Agora associations; B · Structural tractability from CanSAR network percentile and consensus cavity analysis (saturated to 1.000 across this post-screen cohort — not a discriminator at this stage); C · Chemical feasibility from CanSAR chemistry-based percentile blended with Probe Miner exposure; D · Competitive openness derived from the FTO findings and ChEMBL footprint heuristics.

Druggability tier (A–D). Standalone CanSAR network-percentile assignment, separate from the composite. Tier A = ≥75th percentile.

FTO verdicts. Conservative public-source readouts. CLEAR means no material public IP or competitive obstacle was found; WATCH means proceed with monitoring or focused counsel review; BLOCKED means a significant public IP or competitive obstacle was identified. Patent-tier estimates are directional public-source and not a legal validity or infringement opinion.

Probe Miner status. Validated probe = compound flagged suitable_probe = 1; Compounds present = at least one matched compound but none meet probe-quality thresholds; Chemically virgin = zero matched compounds.

Citations. Pass 1 surfaces inline links from the FTO source markdown. Pass 2 will populate per-target scholarly evidence (PubMed by category) and patent evidence (composition-of-matter / antibody / biomarker / delivery).
