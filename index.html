Decisions you can defend, on demand.

Whether you’re advancing a clinical asset or evaluating one for investment, the AI in the loop has to be defensible — to regulators, to LPs, to boards, to the next person who asks “how do you know?”

Foster Rx Systems Inc. builds for that question. Every output ANGIS produces is constructed to four standards:

  • Every intelligence output is cryptographically signed with a public key anyone can verify.
  • Every output carries a provenance chain — what data fed it, what models produced it, when, by whom.
  • Every output is human-reviewed before certification — no unattested AI outputs reach decisions.
  • Every output is reproducible from the same inputs at any later date.

The current alternatives don’t work for regulated decisions.

Generic AI tools — consumer chat assistants, internal LLM wrappers, off-the-shelf model APIs — produce outputs that can’t be audited, can’t be reproduced, and can’t be defended in a regulatory inspection. Sponsors and investors who rely on them carry hidden risk on every decision.

Established clinical intelligence platforms provide curated data feeds but not governed AI analysis. Customers who want both end up integrating the data themselves, with all the AI-output validation problems that creates.

Foster Rx Systems Inc. fills the gap: governed AI infrastructure designed for regulated biopharma from day one.

Built by someone who has lived the problem.

Foster Rx Systems Inc. was founded by Gabrielle Foster, MSPH, MPH, who has spent her career at the intersection of clinical operations, regulated data, and applied data science.

She studied biomedical sciences at Harvard, earned her MSPH from Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health and her MPH from MCPHS, and trained in applied data science at MIT. Her doctoral research is focused on the intersection of healthcare business and technology.

She has run clinical operations on large-scale global immunology trials at Takeda Pharmaceuticals and conducted research at Johns Hopkins Medicine, the VA MAVERIC system, and the Bill & Melinda Gates Medical Research Institute. She is published in the peer-reviewed literature across anesthesia, pediatric medicine, emergency medicine, and clinical research methodology.

She built Foster Rx Systems Inc. because the governance gap she saw in the industry was not going to close on its own.
